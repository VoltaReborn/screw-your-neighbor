Rules

Screw Your Neighbor

Each active player is dealt one card. Aces are low and Kings are high. Try not to have the lowest card at the reveal.

  1. Play starts left of the dealer and moves toward the dealer.
  2. On your turn, choose Stay or Trade with the next player.
  3. If you try to trade with a King, that King flips up and blocks the trade.
  4. If you try to trade with a Queen, that Queen flips up and forces you to trade with the previous player instead.
  5. If that forced backward trade hits another King or Queen, that card flips up, the original card returns home, and no back-and-forth loop happens.
  6. The dealer acts last and may trade with the deck. Deck trades are never blocked by Kings or Queens.
  7. Everyone reveals. The lowest card gets 1 point. Ties for lowest all get a point.
  8. At 3 points, a player is eliminated. If everyone left would be eliminated together, the round is replayed with the same players.
  9. Last player standing wins.
